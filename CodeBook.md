#Data Dictionary - Human Activity Recognition Using Smartphones Dataset tidy Coursera Project

Modified from documentation found at https://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/getdata%2Fprojectfiles%2FUCI%20HAR%20Dataset.zip

* subject
    + An identifier of the subject who performend the activity summarized in the tidy data row. Its range is 1 - 30.
* activity
    + One of the following: WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING
